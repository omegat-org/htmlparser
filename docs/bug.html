Bug Reports

You can submit bug reports here. Before you proceed, please note that you should sign in at sourceforge, for only then will our responses reach you. Putting in an anonymous bug report will provide us with no way of reaching you. If you do not have a sourceforge login, then please consider mentioning your email address in the bug report.

Checklist BEFORE you submit your bug report :

  • Have you checked the list of older bug reports
  • Have you written a testcase to simulate your bug ? Why do we request this - check Communicate with Testcases. We do take reports without testcases, but pls note that such reports may take longer for us to respond to.

Once you are ready, click here to submit your report (upload your testcase as a file attachment).
