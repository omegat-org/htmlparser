Tags and Nodes
 

 
The basic tags supported are shown below in the diagram as children of HTMLTag. Apart from the tags, HTMLEndTag, HTMLStringNode and HTMLRemarkNode are special types of HTMLNode that are identified seperately by the HTMLReader. Only HTMLTag is identified on its own. 
After that, the registered scanners are called to identify which particular tag it is.
